Dear all, if you have arrived at this website, then you have received an invitation to our wedding. Congratulations on your golden ticket! We hope that you are able to celebrate with us.

On this page, you will find information about the schedule, the venue, accommodation, parking and other things surrounding the event.

Dress code: Wear what you want. Come as you are.

Schedule

W-Day - 1: 27th of August:

  • 11:00-17:00: Meet at Excursion at Erimanthos river.
    Hiking, swimming in river & waterfalls, picnic etc. Please bring hiking shoes and a second pair of shoes incase they get wet. Take lots of water and a swimming suit. Approximate hiking time: 1 hour by foot. River accessible by car.

Wedding day: 28th of August:

  • 17:30: The wedding ceremony starts at the Agia Aikaterini church.
  • After: Reception: Dinner & Party at Lala's Village.
